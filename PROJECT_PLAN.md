# Emnex AI Trader
### Professional AI-Powered Market Analysis Platform

---

# Vision

Build an AI-powered trading platform that analyzes financial markets in real-time, automatically draws technical analysis on live charts, explains every decision, and acts like a professional trading mentor.

The platform does NOT aim to blindly generate signals.

Instead, it explains WHY the market is bullish, bearish, or ranging.

Think:

TradingView
+
LuxAlgo
+
AI Trading Mentor
+
Professional Trading Journal
+
Market Scanner

all inside one application.

---

# Primary Goal

Create the most intelligent market analysis platform capable of:

✔ Displaying live charts
✔ Drawing trendlines automatically
✔ Detecting support & resistance
✔ Detecting Supply/Demand
✔ Detecting Swing Highs & Swing Lows
✔ Detecting BOS
✔ Detecting CHoCH
✔ Detecting Market Structure
✔ Calculating probabilities
✔ Suggesting Buy/Sell/Wait
✔ Explaining every decision
✔ Learning from previous trades
